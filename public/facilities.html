Campus Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure designed to foster holistic development and innovative learning.

Smart Classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated classrooms equipped with interactive smart boards, reliable Wi-Fi, and ergonomic furniture to ensure a comfortable and engaging learning environment.

Modern Library

A serene reading space housing over 20,000 volumes, international journals, and a digital section providing access to thousands of e-books and online academic resources.

Advanced Laboratories

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and computer labs where students can conduct practical experiments and explore robotics and coding.

Sports Complex

Extensive outdoor sports fields for football and cricket, alongside indoor courts for basketball, badminton, and table tennis to promote physical fitness.

Transport Fleet

A fleet of highly maintained, GPS-enabled air-conditioned buses ensuring safe and comfortable transit for students commuting across the city.

Hygienic Cafeteria

A spacious dining hall serving nutritious, balanced, and freshly prepared hot meals and snacks for students and staff under strict hygiene standards.
